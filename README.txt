RT Admin
========

Drupal 7 Admin Theme for developers
 - subtheme of seven
 - darker header
 - rounded corners
 - cleaner/tighter header
 - custom maintenance page
 - branded with logo.png
 
 CUSTOMIZE
 ===========
 Replace the logo.png with your own, and you are ready to go. You may also want to rename the folder, .info file,, css/js filenames, and .info contents. But, its up to you.
 
 ROADMAP
 ==========
 1) logo link to site
 2) optional text/html in footer (slogan or link to support form, for example)
